Yoga classes

Gentle Hatha Yoga
Inteded for beginners and anyone wishing a grounded foundation in the practice of uoga, this 60 minute
class of poses and slow movement focus on asana (proper alignment and posture), paranayama (breath work),
and guided meditation to foster your mind and body connection.
Vinyasa Yoga
Although designed for intermediate to advanced students, beginners are welcome to smple this 60 minute
class that focuses on breath-synchronized movement-you will inhale and exhal as you flow energetically
through yoga poses.
Restorative Yoga
This 90 minute class features very slow movemnt and long poses that are supported by a chair or wall.
This calming, restorative experience is suitable for students of any level of experience.
This practice can be a perfect way to help rehabilitate an injury.
